Recipe #1 - Cake

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 5

Have you ever wondered how to make a cake? Well this is your opportunity to learn with this very succinct recipe. In this recipe we are going to make a cake. The good thing with this is that you can modify it to fit your tastebuds.
e.g. You can add cocoa powder to add a chocolate flavour to your recipe!

Ingredients Quantity
Self-raising flour 500g
Butter 500g
Caster Sugar 450g
Eggs 4

Step 1: Get all your ingrediants weighed and measured and placed right in fron of you.

Step 2: First pour in the sugar and butter and cream them together until light and fluffy

Step 3: Then you whisk the eggs together in a separate bowl then add half of the eggs at a time and whisk them immediately. If they begin to kurdle then whisk harder!

Step 4: After that sieve in your flour. It is very important to sieve in your flour as this adds more air to your mixture and helps it to rise. Make sure you also fold in your flour!
Also at this point you can add in any additional flavours

Step 5: Once you have completed all those steps then you can line a baking tray with butter - or just lining! and cook it for around 15-30 mins.

With these 5 easy steps you too can make a delicious cake!!! Once you're done your cake may look something like this:
